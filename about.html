The Basics

I am an electrical engineer who is interested in the world of embedded systems and though I dislike the acronym, IoT. Building systems that inform us about our world and enable us to be smarter about how we live is my goal. After a string of office jobs that I found a strong distaste for, I have moved in my own direction of becoming a freelance engineer. If you like what you see on this site and you've got an idea, feel free to contact me, even you think its a little crazy. Crazy ideas that just might work are what we need these days.
